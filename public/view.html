Question 1.1
Solve for $x$: $2x + 3 = 11$
(2 Marks)

Answer

Final Answer $x = 4$

Step-by-Step Solution

1 Subtract 3 from both sides to isolate the term with $x$.
Step 1
$$2x + 3 = 11$$ $$2x + 3 - 3 = 11 - 3$$ $$2x = 8$$
2 Divide both sides by 2 to solve for $x$.
Step 2
$$2x = 8$$ $$\frac{2x}{2} = \frac{8}{2}$$ $$x = 4$$
3 Therefore, the value of $x$ is 4.
Step 3
$$x = 4$$
Tip

Always balance both sides of the equation when solving for $x$.
