Question 1.1 Solve for $x$:  $2x + 3 = 11$ (2 Marks)

Step-by-Step Explanation

Question 1.1
Solve for $x$:
$$2x + 3 = 11$$
Step 1: Subtract 3 from both sides.
$$2x + 3 - 3 = 11 - 3$$
$$2x = 8$$
Step 2: Divide both sides by 2.
$$\frac{2x}{2} = \frac{8}{2}$$
$$x = 4$$
